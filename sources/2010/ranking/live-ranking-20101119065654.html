Final Rankings for 2010 & Promotions following the Nationals

The final ranking for 2010 have now been published.

The process of complining the ranking is a complicated one which involves merging results. Please check them in particular if your sailor has changed sail number during the course of the season.

Should you believe that an error has been made please contact Michael Mc Cann by emailing optimist@canndoit.ie

Qualification for the 2011 IODAI team trials are based on these rankings.

In the senior fleet, the first 40 sailors after excluding sailors who must compulsorily retire because of their age will qualify.

In the junior fleet, the first 35 sailors will qualify.

In both cases, no account will be taken of voluntary retirements in calculating the top ranked sailors.

This change has been made to ensure that sailors know will know now if they have qualified as  so that they can commit to a training programme for the following year.
