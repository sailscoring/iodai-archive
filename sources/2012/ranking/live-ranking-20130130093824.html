2012 Optimist Senior and Junior Rankings

All 4 regional events and the Nationals have now been completed and the Final 2012 rankings have been published.

Sailors are reminded that the criteria for receiving an invitation to the 2013 trials are as follows:

  • Both Fleets: Sailors count their results from their best two regional events and the Nationals.
  • Senior Fleet: Top 40 (and ties) after taking out compulsory retirements due to age. Note that it is compulsory to have competed at two regional events and the natoinals to qualify for trials.
  • Junior Fleet: Top 40 sailors and ties.
