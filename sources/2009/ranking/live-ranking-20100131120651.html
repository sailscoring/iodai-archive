Rankings September 2009 - Post Leinsters

The national rankings for the senior and junior fleets have been published following the Leinsters.

Please check your ranking as anomalies can occur particularly if you have changed sail number during the year.

Note that the rankings are based on the sailors best two places in the regionals plus their place in the Nationals. There is no additional weighting for the Nationals.

The changes to the ranking system were published on the website before the start of the season as part of a comprehensive newsletter here and further explained in a specific note on the ranking system here.
