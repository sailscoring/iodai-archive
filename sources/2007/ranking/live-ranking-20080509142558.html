Rankings January 2008

These are the latest rankings as of 20th February on which the trials invitation will be based.

The top 70 sailors will be invited to the trials.  Invitations will be sent by post shortly.

The IODAI Committee have decided to grant a special dispensation to sailor Katie Matthews based on her results in The Nationals and ONE regional.  She was unable to compete in the final Regional Championships as a result of a broken arm.  If any other sailor believes they deserve a similar dispensation they should submit thier case to honsec@iodai.com ASAP for consideration by the committee.
