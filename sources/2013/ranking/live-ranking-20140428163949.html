Final Rankings 2013

                            

Senior Fleet Final Ranking

Junior Fleet Final Rankings 2013

 

All 4 regional events and the Nationals have now been completed and the Final 2013 rankings have been published.

Sailors are reminded that the criteria for receiving an invitation to the 2014 trials are as follows:

  • Both Fleets: Sailors count their results from their best two regional events and the Nationals.
  • Senior Fleet: Top 40 (and ties) after taking out compulsory retirements due to age. 
  • Junior Fleet: Top 40 sailors and ties.
  • Note that it is compulsory to have competed at two regional events and the  Nationals to qualify for trials.
