Rankings February 2009

All sailors and parents are advised to read the previous article in conjunction with these rankings.  Click here: http://www.iodai.com/live/579.html

An additional 3 junior sailors will be invited to the trials i.e. those junior sailors ranked 36th; 37th; and 38th.  These sailors are invited based purely on numerical calculations of alternative publicised criteria.  Again the explanation is contained in http://www.iodai.com/live/579.html

In the senior fleet, the application of different criteria made no difference to the top 35 ranked sailors.  Therefore only 35 Seniors will be invited to the trials.
